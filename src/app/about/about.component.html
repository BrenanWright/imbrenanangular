brief summary of me

I graduated from the University of Alabama with a degree in Management Information Systems in May 2019 and I am currently employed at Innovid as a Technical Services Engineer. At Innovid I am responsible for handling a ticketing system for users of our advertising platform. My day to day consists of troubleshooting XML/JSON, managing data queries using MySQL and Postgres, and developing tools to help our platform work better. I am an intuitive problem solver who loves to create. This love of creating extends from myself into writing, photography, film, and programming. I see programming as the ultimate middle-ground between the right brain and the left brain. To logically work out problems gets the problem-solving left-brain neurons firing, and outputting something that is visible and tangible gets my creative right-brain neurons firing; I absolutely love coding and I thrive in it. I have learned many different coding languages during my studies and professional life such as C#, Java, C, Python, Javascript, and a myriad of different ways to manipulate those and create software projects such as Angular, .NET Core, Django and more. I wore many hats on different projects during my time at Alabama including project manager, tech lead, designer, and developer.

Other than what is essentially my cover letter, I have deep love of the arts. I think there is no limit to what art can express and I take a lot of pride in creating, whether it be photography, web-design, writing, theatre, or film. I am an absolute film-enthusiast, and I could beat almost anyone at film trivia! I hope some day to leverage these creative interests into being involved in the filmmaking process as a career, either in screenwriting or directing.
